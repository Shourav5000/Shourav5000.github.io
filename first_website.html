By : Shourav Mandal アニメの恋人




- Introduction はじめに -


Hi, This is Shourav and I am an Anime Sketch Artist. I started sketching anime during this time of the Covid19 pandemic as a hobby. Fortunately, as time went by and the world still shut. I transformed my art as a part of my daily routine. I sketch often, mostly for myself and my friends. Sometimes, I draw other people as anime characters, as it sometimes brings joy in their eyes to see themselves as an animated character. Makes me happy as well.

I like to sketch, as it helps me focus and relax my mind from other things that might cause me depression or stress. Because after the work is done, I am always proud of what I created through my imagination and love for anime. Even Though it might not end up being what I wanted it to be. In the end its fun and I think I can do a lot more by sharing my art work to the world.





Please Enjoy Sketch Anime! ^_^ Your Friendly Animator. 優しいアニメーター
